Restaurant Description:
Seafare Food is a classic New England fresh seafood restaurant. Our food is sourced via local fisherman that focus primarily on the gulf of Maine for their catches. Seafare Food can accomodate a number of food allergies and offers additional non-seafood options to ensure everyone one in your party enjoys their time with us!
