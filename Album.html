Count Me In

Rebelution

2007

Count Me In embodies the spirit of Rebelution. With hits like Count Me in, De-Stress, and Roots Reggae Music the band embraces their good vibes mantra and the album is full of big brass solo's with signifcant trumpet-sax duels.
